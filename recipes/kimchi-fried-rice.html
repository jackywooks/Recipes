Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Kimchi Fried Rice is a Korean popular dish

The sour and spicy flavour of Kimchi is its irresistable key.

Ingredient

  • Kimchi 150
  • Any Kind of Meat 150-200g
  • Cooked Rice 500g
  • Sesame Oiled Seaweed
  • Spice and condiment
    • Coarse Chili Powder 1 tablespoon
    • Fine Chili Powder 1 tablespoon
    • Sesame Oil 1 tablespoon (Optional)

Steps

  1. Add oil and wait until the oil reach its shimmering point
  2. lower the heat and fry the meat in medium heat in a pot until it is brown
  3. Add Soya Sauce toast the meat a little bit until you smell the flavour of the soya sauce evaporate
  4. Place the Kimchi in a bowl and cut them into finger head size and add them to the pot
  5. bring the heat up a bit
  6. Add the rice in and stir until the rice and the kimchi liquid mixed well
  7. Add one tablesppon of coarse chilli powder and one tablespoon of fine chilli powder
  8. Add a full spoon of sesame oil to ensure the rice is smooth
  9. Add and mix sesame seaweed and mixed well
  10. Plate it
  11. Fried a Sunny-sided egg and place it on top of the fried rice
  12. Sprinkle a few more sesame seaweed on top and viola
