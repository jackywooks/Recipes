Kimchi JJigae (Kimchi Stew)

Description

Kimchi Jjigae or Kimchi Stew is a traditional Korean dish.

The sour and spicy flavour of Kimchi is its irresistable key.

Ingredient

  • Kimchi 200g
  • Any Kind of Meat 150-200g
  • Shimeji Mushroom 100g
  • Tofu 200g
  • Spice and condiment
    • Coarse Chili Powder 1 tablespoon
    • Fine Chili Powder 1 tablespoon
    • Fish Juice 1 tablespoon (Optional)
    • Soya Sauce 1 tablespoon (Optional)

Steps

  1. Add oil and wait until the oil reach its shimmering point
  2. lower the heat and fry the meat in medium heat in a pot until it is brown
  3. Add Shimeji mushroom and brown it as well
  4. Add Soya Sauce toast the meat a little bit until you smell the flavour of the soya sauce evaporate
  5. Place the Kimchi in a bowl and cut them into finger head size and add them to the pot
  6. Add boiled Water and bring the heat up, keep stirring until it is boil
  7. Add one tablesppon of coarse chilli powder and one tablespoon of fine chilli powder
  8. Add one tablespoon of Fish sauce if you have any
  9. Taste and add salt if necessary. If it is too spicy, try to add a little bit of sugar or white vingear to balance it
  10. turn the fire to medium and keep it cook for 10 minutes
  11. Add tofa slice, gently stir the stew to prevent the tofa break and let it cook for 5 more minutes
  12. And you can taste the Korean authentic Kimchi JJigae!
